Alahji Umaru Musa Yar'adua

1951-2010

One Of Most Admired and best president Nigeria has ever produced

Umaru Musa Yar'adua was born on the 16 August 1951 and passed on 5th of May, 2010. Yar'adua is an Hausa Fulani by tribe. Born into a titled Fulani family in Katsina. He is a leader, Teacher and a truthful and Honest person.
